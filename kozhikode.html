Kozhikode

Kozhikode is a coastal city in the south Indian state of Kerala. It was a significant spice trade center and is close to Kappad Beach, where Portuguese explorer Vasco da Gama landed in 1498. The central Kozhikode Beach, overlooked by an old lighthouse, is a popular spot for watching the sunset. Inland, tree-lined Mananchira Square, with its musical fountain, surrounds the massive Mananchira Tank, an artificial pond.

Hotspots

Beypore Beach

Beypore beach is a combination of a sea beach, a promenade cum waterbreak and the signature backwaters of Kerala. The beach is long and clean with beautiful waves christening it's shores.

S.M Street

This place is known as Mittai Theruvu or SM Street is one of the most popular and historic trade centers in calicut. This is one of the most busiest streets and Shopping spots in Calicut.

Kappad Beach

This is one of the best beaches in Kerala and is located a bit far from Calicut city, free from the hustle and bustle of the city life. Definitely a must visit place in Calicut. It has a Historical story behind this beach, Vasco da gama (British).
